About RED

RED The Band

 

RED was founded in May 2023 by three friends: Reza, Efti, and Dipto. Each coming from different musical tastes, academic backgrounds, and institutions. The name RED carries a simple meaning: it is crafted from the initials of the founding members who shared one vision.

Although each of them had past band experiences, RED was born from a desire to begin a new journey, one focused on creating and performing their own original music. They had songs written, ideas formed, and a burning urge to share their sound with the world. What they needed was a complete, committed team.

In the beginning, RED performed with guest instrumentalists while searching for permanent members. They played a few gigs and gave audiences a glimpse of their potential. Still, without the right chemistry, they couldn’t fully express the strength of their music. Eventually, the project went on pause.

But passion has its way of returning stronger.

In time, they found musicians who shared the same drive, dreams, and dedication. People who saw RED not just as a band, but as an investment of heart and purpose. Despite having different musical tastes, their creative wavelengths aligned, and the RED that exists today was born.

REDWAN REZA

 

One of the voices and pen behind RED’s songs. As both a vocalist and lyricist, he channels raw emotion into every lyric and pours everything into the mic. On stage, it’s not just singing—every word, every scream or whisper, comes from something real. For him, music isn’t a performance; it’s an inseparable part of who he is.

AHMED EFTI

 The drummer of the band, dedicated to improving his craft every single day. Constantly practicing new techniques and refining his skills, he is always looking for ways to bring more to the band’s sound. His commitment to growth not only elevates his own performance but also strengthens the overall energy and impact of the group.

SAMIN YASIR DIPTO

He isn’t just another vocalist in RED— he is someone born for music. His passion runs deep, and it shows in every note he sings. Emotion, precision, and raw honesty shape his voice, revealing a connection to music that feels instinctive and alive.

But his talent doesn’t end there. As a guitarist and lyricist, he brings ideas, melodies, and stories to the band. His guitar reflects his emotions, and his writing gives shape to the feelings he cannot say out loud.

For him, music isn’t a hobby or a dream— it is his identity. It drives him, defines him, and pushes him to create. Every performance proves one thing: music is his life, and he pours all of it into RED.

AOYON ROY

The band’s rhythm guitarist, producer, and sound engineer, with 17 years of traditional music training and 7 years in band music. Playfully known as “Goriber Bassbaba” he keeps learning every day from the artists he admires. Constantly refining his skills, he aims to give the band—and the audience—something fresh and unforgettable. He doesn’t know where the journey will lead, but he takes it all in stride. 

ATM SHADMAN ANAM A.K.A (SADMAN DIBBO)

 

The band’s lead guitarist. At 25, he has been on a 16-year journey with a six-string guitar and shows no signs of stopping. In the band, he keeps things chill while delivering his lead guitar skills and recording when needed, bringing both energy and experience to the group.

KOWSHIK RAHMAN

 The band’s bassist. Music has opened a new horizon in his life, introducing him to new sounds and genres that continue to inspire him. He has been playing bass for a year, constantly learning and finding new ways to improve his skills.

Mustakim Swad

 

The Band's Photographer. 

He began not as a “photographer,” but as someone who simply loved capturing moments. His journey started at college concerts, where he documented memories rather than events. But things truly took off during a college football tournament, when the shots he captured on his Android phone exceeded everyone’s expectations.

What began as a casual hobby quickly became a passion worth pursuing. His turning point came at the University Freshers’ Reception, where RED noticed his spontaneous, vibrant captures and instantly connected with his style.

From that moment onward, the path was set and the rest is history.

Serene Jannat

Serene joined RED as their manager in 2025 with the same passion and ambition that shaped the band from the beginning. With a strong background in organization, strategy, and understanding artist needs, Serene manages the band's operations, schedules, and outreach.
