About RED

 

RED was founded in May 2023 by three friends: Reza, Efti, and Dipto. Each coming from different musical tastes, academic backgrounds, and institutions. The name RED carries a simple meaning: it is crafted from the initials of the founding members who shared one vision.

Although each of them had past band experiences, RED was born from a desire to begin a new journey, one focused on creating and performing their own original music. They had songs written, ideas formed, and a burning urge to share their sound with the world. What they needed was a complete, committed team.

In the beginning, RED performed with guest instrumentalists while searching for permanent members. They played a few gigs and gave audiences a glimpse of their potential. Still, without the right chemistry, they couldn’t fully express the strength of their music. Eventually, the project went on pause.

But passion has its way of returning stronger.

In time, they found musicians who shared the same drive, dreams, and dedication. People who saw RED not just as a band, but as an investment of heart and purpose. Despite having different musical tastes, their creative wavelengths aligned, and the RED that exists today was born.

Mustakim Swad

 

The Band's Photographer. 

He began not as a “photographer,” but as someone who simply loved capturing moments. His journey started at college concerts, where he documented memories rather than events. But things truly took off during a college football tournament, when the shots he captured on his Android phone exceeded everyone’s expectations.

What began as a casual hobby quickly became a passion worth pursuing. His turning point came at the University Freshers’ Reception, where RED noticed his spontaneous, vibrant captures and instantly connected with his style.

From that moment onward, the path was set and the rest is history.
